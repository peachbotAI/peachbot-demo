## Not Medical Advice

- This system does **not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment**
- It is **not a certified medical device**
- It is **not approved by any regulatory authority** (e.g., FDA, CE, HSA Singapore)
- All data used in this demo is **synthetic and simulated**

---

## Intended Use

PeachBot is designed as a **clinical decision support concept**:

- Supports healthcare professionals — does not replace them  
- Provides insights — does not make decisions  
- Requires human validation for any interpretation  

---

## Safety Philosophy

The system follows a **human-in-the-loop approach**:

- AI outputs are **constrained and explainable**
- All decisions remain under **human control**
- Designed for **transparency and auditability**
